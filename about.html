About Me

Hi, I am Braydyn. I am a Junior in Computer Science at the University of Idaho. Thoughout various years of learning computer science i have found a special passion for video game development as well as software engineering.

I have working experience with C, C++, C#, Python, and Java. I specialize in developing software ranging from Low Level Calculators to High Level Game Design. My focus area is Game Development & Software Engineering. Ive developed various projects including Simulation Games, Platformer, and 3d Games, ive also created a linux command line simulator that processes commands similar to a linux system.

Outside of coding, I’ve have had the chance to take leadership roles on campus, where I have developed skills in teamwork, management, and mentoring peers. As the President of Game Devs United, I’ve helped other students start working on their video game ideas , I’ve also worked on collaborative development projects. Additionally, my experience in the Idaho Army National Guard has strengthened my discipline, adaptability, and teamwork.

Beyond that i enjoy playing instruments such as the violin and guitar. I also enjoy working on creative projects such as woodworking Camping etc. This sort of thing helps me develop different environments in games.
